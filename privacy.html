Privacy Policy

Rize: Alarm Challenge. Effective date: July 11, 2026

The short version: Rize runs no servers, shows no ads, and does no tracking. Your data stays on your iPhone and in your personal iCloud, which only you can access. We cannot see, sell, or share your data, because it never reaches us.

Who we are

Rize is developed by Efe Aksoy (the "developer", "we"). Contact: efeaksoy9445@gmail.com.

Your account (Sign in with Apple)

Rize requires Sign in with Apple. When you sign in, Apple gives the app a stable, anonymous user identifier and, only on your first sign-in, your name and email address if you choose to share them. These are stored on your device (the identifier in the device Keychain) and are used to greet you and show your account in Settings. They are never transmitted to the developer or to any third party.

What the app stores

  • Alarms and preferences: stored on your device and synced through your private iCloud (CloudKit) so your other devices see them.
  • Streaks, profile name, and settings: stored on your device and synced through your private iCloud key-value storage.
  • Profile photo: stored only as a file on your device. It is never uploaded anywhere and is not synced.

iCloud data lives in your personal, private iCloud account. The developer has no technical ability to read it. Apple's own privacy terms govern iCloud.

Camera

Two wake-up challenges can use the camera, with your permission:

  • Photo challenge: the photo you take is analyzed on your device to check the object, then discarded. It is not saved to your library and never leaves your device.
  • Push-up challenge: the front camera shows a live self-view and counts your push-ups using on-device face detection. Frames are analyzed in memory and immediately discarded. Nothing is recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Subscriptions and payments

Rize Pro is an auto-renewable subscription processed entirely by Apple through your Apple account. We never see your payment details. Manage or cancel anytime in Settings, then your name, then Subscriptions on your device.

Analytics, advertising, and tracking

None. Rize contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising, and no trackers, and it does not share data with third parties.

Deleting your data

In the app, open Settings and choose Delete Account. This permanently removes your alarms, streaks, profile, and settings from the device and from your private iCloud, and signs you out. You can also revoke Rize's Sign in with Apple access in your Apple account settings.

Children

Rize is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly store personal information beyond what is described above.
