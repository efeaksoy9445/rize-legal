Privacy Policy for Rize

Last updated: 29 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Rize app ("Rize", "the app", "we", "us") handles information when you use it. Rize is provided by Efe Aksoy ("the developer"). By using Rize, you agree to the practices described here.

1. The short version

Rize is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not operate servers that collect your personal data, we do not sell or share your information, and we do not use third-party advertising or tracking. Your alarms and settings stay on your iPhone.

2. Information stored on your device

Rize stores the alarms and preferences you create (such as alarm times, labels, repeat days, chosen challenge type, and difficulty) locally on your device. This information is not transmitted to the developer and is not accessible to us. If you delete the app, this locally stored information is removed with it.

3. Information we do not collect

Rize does not collect your name, email address, location, contacts, photos, health data, or any analytics about how you use the app. We do not build a profile of you and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

4. Subscriptions and payments

Rize offers an optional paid subscription ("Rize Pro"). All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store using your Apple ID. The developer never receives or stores your payment card details. Apple handles billing, renewals, and refunds in accordance with Apple's own terms and privacy policy. To understand how Apple processes this information, please review Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. System permissions

Rize requests permission to schedule alarms on your device (via Apple's AlarmKit). This permission is used solely to ring alarms you have set. It does not grant the developer any access to your data.

6. Children

Rize is not directed at children under the age required for consent in their country, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app does not collect personal data at all, no special children's data is gathered.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes take effect means you accept the revised policy.
