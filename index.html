Privacy

FAQ

Fair questions

How does Rize stop me from sleeping through alarms?

When your alarm rings, you tap Solve to open a challenge. The alarm only truly stops once you complete it. Leave early and it rings right back about a minute later.

What are the challenge types?

Math, Memory, Photo, and Push-ups. Difficulty scales how hard each one is, from gentle to ruthless. Photo, Push-ups, and the Ruthless level are part of Rize Pro.

Do you collect my data?

No servers, no ads, no tracking. Your data stays on your device and in your private iCloud. Camera challenges run fully on device and nothing is recorded.

Can I try Pro for free?

Yes, both plans start with a 1-week free trial. Cancel anytime from your Apple account settings.
