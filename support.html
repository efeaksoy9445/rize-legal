Frequently asked questions

How does Rize stop me from sleeping through my alarm?

When your alarm rings, you complete a challenge — math, memory, or typing — to turn it off. If you leave without finishing, the alarm rings again a short time later, so you can't dismiss it half-asleep.

The alarm rang again after I dismissed it. Is that a bug?

No — that's intended. If you tap Stop or leave before completing the challenge, Rize re-arms the alarm so it rings again shortly. Completing the challenge is what fully turns it off.

What are the different challenges?

Math (solve an equation), Memory (repeat a pattern), and Typing (retype a phrase). Difficulty can be set from easy to hard.

How do streaks and freezes work?

Complete a challenge each day to build your streak. If you miss a day, a freeze automatically protects your streak. You start with a couple of freezes.

What does Rize Pro include, and how do I manage it?

Rize Pro unlocks the typing challenge, unlimited alarms, and hard difficulty. It's an auto-renewable subscription billed through your Apple ID. You can manage or cancel it anytime in your device's Settings → your name → Subscriptions. You can also restore a previous purchase from the paywall or Settings → About → Restore Purchases.

Do you collect my data?

No. Your alarms, settings, name, and streaks stay on your device. Rize has no accounts, no servers, and no tracking.

An alarm didn't go off. What should I do?

Make sure Rize has alarm permission enabled (iOS Settings → Rize), that your device isn't in a mode that silences alarms, and that the alarm is toggled on in the app. If problems continue, email us at the address above and we'll help.
