Common questions

  • The alarm did not ring: check that Rize has alarm permission (iPhone Settings, Rize) and that the phone is on and charged.
  • Push-ups are not counted: prop the phone up so your face is clearly in the frame, and use a full range of motion. Without camera permission, lay the phone flat and bring your nose to the screen each rep.
  • Manage or cancel Rize Pro: iPhone Settings, your name, Subscriptions, or in the app under the Pro tab, Manage subscription.
  • Delete your account: in the app, Settings tab, Delete Account at the bottom. This removes all data from the device and your iCloud.
