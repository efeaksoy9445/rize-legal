What Rize is

Rize is an alarm app that asks you to complete a wake-up challenge (for example solving math problems, repeating a pattern, photographing an object, or doing push-ups) before an alarm can be dismissed. An Apple account sign-in is required to use the app.

Important: alarm reliability

Alarms depend on your device: it must be powered on, not in a mode that silences alarms, and the app must have alarm permission. Software, hardware, or system conditions outside our control can prevent an alarm from ringing. Do not rely on Rize as your only alarm for critical situations (flights, exams, medication, work shifts). Use a backup where missing a wake-up would cause harm or loss.
